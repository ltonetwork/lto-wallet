How does it work?

The bridge will charge a fee for transfering your tokens. This fee is a standard fee of {{ bridgeFee }} LTO, which will withdrawn from your transfered tokens.

To help you understand the process of swapping LTO (ERC-20) to LTO (MAINNET), we have made an example:

The bridge will charge a fee for transfering your tokens. This fee will be burned to lower the total amount of tokens in circulating supply. The height of this bridge fee decreases over time.
